# Inventory Manager / Inventory Controller — Epic-Based User Story Backlog

## Epic Overview

| Epic ID | Epic Name                                                   | Epic Goal                                                                                                    |
| ------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| I1      | Vision Inventory Capture & Stock Reconciliation             | Enable fast stock scanning, physical-vs-system matching, shrink detection, and inventory validation.         |
| I2      | Smart Receiving, ASN & Transfer Engine                      | Automate receiving, ASN matching, put-away, inter-store transfers, and vendor returns.                       |
| I3      | Cycle Count, Variance & Audit Control                       | Maintain inventory accuracy through scheduled counts, variance workflows, signoffs, and investigations.      |
| I4      | Product Authentication & Serialization                      | Create a permanent Product Digital Twin for every luxury item with serial number, COA, and provenance trail. |
| I5      | Service Logistics & Loaner Tracking                         | Track items moving to service centers and manage loaner inventory linked to After-Sales Tickets.             |
| I6      | Inventory Intelligence Dashboard & Predictive Replenishment | Give managers real-time inventory health, stockout risk, overstock alerts, and replenishment suggestions.    |
